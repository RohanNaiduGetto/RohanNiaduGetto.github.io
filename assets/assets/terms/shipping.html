SHIPPING POLICY

Last updated January 15, 2025

This Shipping Policy for Getovity Technologies LLP (doing business as Getto) ("we," "us," or "our"), describes how we are committed to providing fast, reliable, and convenient delivery services to our customers. Please review our comprehensive shipping policy below for detailed information on delivery times, areas, costs, and other relevant conditions.

1. Delivery Time

Standard Delivery

Our goal is to deliver your orders within 60 minutes for local deliveries within 5kms. Our quick-commerce model is designed to ensure that your items reach you as swiftly as possible. However, due to unforeseen circumstances, delivery times may extend up to 4 hours in the following situations:

Adverse weather conditions

High traffic volume or roadblocks

Public holidays or local restrictions

Unavailability of delivery personnel

In such cases, we will promptly notify you and keep you updated on the status of your order through our app or via email/SMS.

Special Deliveries

For bulk orders or special items, delivery times may vary. We will provide a specific estimated delivery time during the order process.

2. Delivery Area

Serviceable Locations

We currently offer delivery services within specific geographic zones. The availability of delivery to your address will be determined during the checkout process. Please ensure your address is within our serviceable areas before placing an order.

Expansion Plans

We are actively working to expand our delivery zones to serve more customers. Keep an eye on future updates as we introduce new serviceable locations.

Non-Delivery Areas

If your address is outside our current delivery area, we recommend checking back periodically as we continue to grow our reach.

3. Shipping Costs

Shipping fees are dynamically calculated based on several factors:

Distance between the delivery location and the nearest local store or warehouse.

Special delivery requests, if applicable.

The most accurate shipping fee will be displayed at checkout before completing your purchase.

Non-Refundable Shipping Fees

Please note that shipping costs are non-refundable once the order has been dispatched, even in the event of delivery delays.

4. Order Tracking

Once your order is placed, you will receive a confirmation message with an estimated delivery time. You can track your order in real-time through our app or website.

Tracking Updates

Regular updates on your order status, including dispatch, transit, and delivery completion, will be provided to keep you informed every step of the way.

5. Delivery Conditions

Correct Address

Ensure that the delivery address and contact information provided are accurate. We are not responsible for failed deliveries due to incorrect or incomplete information.

Delivery Acceptance

Someone must be present at the delivery address to receive the package. If the delivery cannot be completed due to the recipient's absence, we may attempt a re-delivery, which may incur additional fees.

Failed Delivery Attempts

If delivery cannot be completed after reasonable attempts, the order may be returned to the vendor. Any additional delivery costs incurred will be the customer's responsibility.

6. Modification of Orders

Orders cannot be modified once they have been placed. If changes are needed, we recommend canceling the existing order (if it has not yet been dispatched) and placing a new one.

7. Cancellations

You may cancel your order before it is dispatched for delivery. Once the order has been dispatched, cancellations are no longer possible. Shipping fees are non-refundable for cancellations after dispatch.

8. Delivery Delays

While we strive to deliver within the specified timeframe, unforeseen events may cause delays. In such cases:

Communication: We will inform you immediately and provide a revised estimated delivery time.

Compensation: Delays exceeding 24 hours may make you eligible for a full refund of the order, excluding shipping costs.

9. Changes to the Shipping Policy

We reserve the right to revise and update this shipping policy at any time. Changes will take effect immediately upon posting on our website. It is your responsibility to review the policy before making a purchase to stay informed of any updates.

10. Contact Us

For questions, concerns, or assistance regarding your order or our shipping policy, please contact our customer support team:

Email: contact@gettoindia.com

Hours of Operation: Monday to Sunday, 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM

Thank you for choosing us for your delivery needs. We are dedicated to providing the best service possible.
